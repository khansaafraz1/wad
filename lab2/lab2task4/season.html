Seasons

Spring

One of the four seasons, spring is often considered as an era of rebirth and development.
The spring season provides longer days, higher temperatures, and an increase of activity after the calm, chilly winter months.
Animals become more active, flowers blossom in brilliant colors, and trees and vegetation begin to grow new leaves.
Just like nature is waking up and beginning again, this season feels energizing and new.

Summer

Summer is the hottest of four seasons, and is often linked to warmth, sunshine, and a feeling of freedom.
The days are long, the nights are short, and there is a sense of energy in the air. Temperatures rise, the sun shines brilliantly, and people prefer
to spend more time outside, whether it's in a park, beside a pool, or at the beach.
Flowers are in full bloom, trees are green with life, and the sounds of singing birds, buzzing insects, and laughter fill the air.
